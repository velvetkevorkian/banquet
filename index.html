Brainstorming Ideas

Using mind mapping and card sorting to explore concepts and focus our thinking. What message do we want to convey, and how?

Prototyping

Our aim was to represent the ingredients present in everyday foodstuffs.

Prototype no.1

Layers of raw ingredients give a visual representation of what's in our food. Who knew that ketchup had so much celery in it?

Prototype no.1: Two days later :(

Unfortunately, it didn't age particularly well. How can we prevent it from decaying, and keep the separation between layers of different ingredients?

Beginning prototype no.2: eggs in jelly

Suspending ingredients in jelly might give us a way of separating layers and maintaining an interesting aesthetic

Making

Moving on from just ketchup to include mustard, mayonnaise, tartare sauce and brown sauce. Layering jelly to represent different ingredients, and including a sample of the ingredient where possible.

Labelling

Relabelling to reflect the new contents: deconstructed, stripped of their processing, only the essence remains.

Results!

We aim to illustrate what many people take for granted: the ingredients of many kinds of foods. It provides an interesting visual experience to complement these commonly used condiments.
